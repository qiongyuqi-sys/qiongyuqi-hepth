Research Interests

My research interests are in AdS/CFT correspondence, von Neumann algebra and dS quantum gravity. I used to focus on the entanglement properties of dynamical black hole and the holographic interpretation of dynamical black hole entropy. Recently, I'm focusing on von Neumann algebra and its application in rotating spacetime, we are trying to find how the rotational information encoded in the algebric structure.
