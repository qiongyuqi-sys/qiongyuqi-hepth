Seminar and Workshop

I collaborated with the successor ministers to organize the first, the second and the third workshop on mathematics and theoretical physics. This student-organized initiative seeks to facilitate interinstitutional academic exchange and will feature a diverse array of presentations encompassing topics such as Cosmology, Quantum field theory, Quantum information, Quantum gravity, and various topics in Mathematics.
